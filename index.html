Tribute - A Peek into My World

by Avantika Satheesh

Welcome to My Space 🌸

Hi, I'm Avantika! This little webpage is my tribute - A cozy corner of the internet about the things that make me the happiest: books, crochet, and running. Through this project, I've learned how to build and design my own webpage, and I hope to inspire others to create theirs too!

My Favorite Things

  • 🧶 Crochet: Stitch by stitch, it helps me unwind and create something warm and wonderful
  • 📚 Reading: Books are my quiet escape - they take me to different worlds and teach me something new every time
  • 🏃 Running: Running gives me energy and clears my mind - it's like a moving meditation.

My Web Journey 🌐

Before this, I have never made a website before! I started with simple <html> and <css> code, learning how to style and structure a page. Each tag felt like a new discovery, and seeing it come alive in the browser was so rewarding!

Why This Matters

I made this tribute as part of the Athena Award's "Tribute" project - a way to learn, create, and share. To me, it's proof that anyone can learn to code and express themselves online, one small step (or one crochet loop!) at a time.

My Reflection 💭

Building this page taught me that creativity and code go hand in hand. Just like crochet pattern or a running route, a website has movement, flow, and design. I'm proud of what I've made and can't wait to build even more!
